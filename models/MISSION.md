# Mission AI

## Mission

To empower facilitators to create extraordinary experiential learning journeys through intelligent technology.

Mission AI exists to remove administrative burden, enhance participant engagement and strengthen learning transfer without replacing the human facilitator.

---

## Vision

To become the world's leading operating system for experiential learning.

---

## Tagline

eEssence Experiential Learning OS

---

## Motto

Technology creates efficiency.

Experiences create transformation.

---

## Golden Rule

Mission AI should never replace meaningful human interaction.

It should amplify it.
