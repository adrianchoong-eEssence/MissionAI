# Product Principles

Version 1.0

---

## Our Philosophy

Mission AI exists to create extraordinary experiences through technology.

Technology should never become the experience.

Technology should make the experience possible.

---

# Principle 1

Facilitators come first.

If a feature makes facilitation harder,
it is the wrong feature.

---

# Principle 2

Participants should never need training to use Mission AI.

If instructions are required,
the interface is too complicated.

---

# Principle 3

One screen.

One purpose.

Every screen should answer only one question.

Examples

Registration
→ Have I joined?

Group Formation
→ Which team am I in?

Mission
→ What should I do?

Debrief
→ What should I reflect on?

---

# Principle 4

Remove friction.

Every unnecessary tap should be removed.

Every unnecessary form should disappear.

---

# Principle 5

Facilitators should never repeat work.

If information has already been entered once,
Mission AI should remember it.

---

# Principle 6

Participants should look at people,
not their phones.

Mission AI should encourage interaction.

Never replace interaction.

---

# Principle 7

Every notification must create value.

No meaningless messages.

No notification spam.

---

# Principle 8

Every Programme Block must support learning.

Even Registration.

Even Lunch.

Even Closing.

---

# Principle 9

Everything should be reusable.

Activities.

Teams.

Challenges.

AI prompts.

Programme Blocks.

---

# Principle 10

Mission AI is invisible.

When participants remember the programme,

they should remember

the people,

the learning,

the emotions,

the facilitator.

Not the software.

---

# Success

Mission AI succeeds when facilitators forget they are using software.
